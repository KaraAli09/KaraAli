Ceyda Senay Güler

Psychologist
Kadir Has University
Short Bio

Ceyda Senay Güler is a recent graduate of Kadir Has University in Istanbul, where she earned her Bachelor of Arts in Psychology with a minor in Media Psychology. Her academic journey also included an Erasmus+ exchange program at Fontys University in the Netherlands, where she explored future work dynamics and relationship psychology. Throughout her studies, Ceyda gained extensive experience in both clinical and research settings. She worked as a research assistant in the Transdiagnostic Risk Factors Lab, focusing on data analysis and literature reviews, and at PLAN Psychological & Academic Consulting, where she contributed to parent-child relationship studies and funding applications, including TÜBİTAK. Her clinical experience spans Istanbul University Medical Faculty Hospital, where she assisted in outpatient consultations, and Neşve Psychology, where she specialized in Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT) and its integration into Türkiye's psychological community.

Ceyda's academic contributions include co-authoring a TÜBİTAK project report and publishing an article on gender and public space in Coremag. She presented her work at international conferences, including Radboud University’s Highlights in the Language Sciences Conference 2024.
